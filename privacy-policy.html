Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  CoachAnywhere (ABN to be inserted)

CoachAnywhere is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe — in plain English.

1. Who We Are

CoachAnywhere is operated by CoachAnywhere Pty Ltd (ABN to be inserted), based in Australia. We connect athletes with coaches through our online platform and mobile application.

We are bound by the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at kane@coachanywhere247.com

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you sign up or use the platform:

Information Why we collect it
Name, email, mobile To create and manage your account and communicate with you
Age To verify eligibility and apply parental consent requirements for under 18s
Sport, skill level, location To match you with appropriate coaches and personalise your experience
Main goal To help coaches understand your training objectives
Guardian details (if under 18) To obtain parental consent and provide a safety contact
Video content To deliver AI analysis and coaching feedback
Payment information Processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store card details
Usage data and device info To improve platform performance and user experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your CoachAnywhere account
  • Connect you with coaches and facilitate coaching relationships
  • Deliver AI-powered video analysis and coaching feedback
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you service-related communications and important updates
  • Improve and develop the CoachAnywhere platform
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Video Content and AI Analysis

Videos you upload are shared only with the coach you have subscribed to. Our AI analyses your movement — joints, angles and timing — to assist your coach in delivering precise feedback. This analysis data is not shared outside your coaching relationship.

You retain full ownership of your video content at all times. You may request deletion of your videos by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

CoachAnywhere is available to athletes aged 13 and over. Athletes under 18 must provide verifiable parental or guardian consent at signup. We collect guardian contact details specifically to fulfil this requirement.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have done so in error, please contact us immediately at kane@coachanywhere247.com and we will delete the information promptly.

6. Who We Share Your Information With

We share your information only where necessary:

  • Your coach — receives your profile details and videos to deliver feedback
  • Payment processors — to handle subscription payments securely
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — to store and deliver the platform
  • Analytics providers — to help us understand and improve platform usage

All third-party providers are required to handle your data in accordance with Australian privacy law.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no internet transmission is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your CoachAnywhere account.

8. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kane@coachanywhere247.com

9. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and analyse how the platform is used. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some platform functionality.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of any material changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of CoachAnywhere after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact and Complaints

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at kane@coachanywhere247.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au
