1-on-1 Session Reviews
Upload one of your own coaching sessions or a video of you coaching athletes. Your mentor reviews it, annotates with the same tools coaches use for athletes, and sends back detailed feedback on your technique and approach.

Join your mentor's weekly group session with up to 8 coaches. Discuss coaching philosophy, review game tape together, work through difficult athlete situations and build your network. Max 8 coaches per group for an intimate experience.
